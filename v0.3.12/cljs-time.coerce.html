cljs-time.coerce

### Utilites to coerce goog.data DateTime instances to and from
various other types.

For example, to convert a goog.date DateTime to and from a js number:

  => (to-long (date-time 1998 4 25))
  893462400000

  => (from-long 893462400000)
  #<19980425T000000>
